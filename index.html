From Mat to Mission

At VAISHVA YOG, we believe that yoga is more than a physical practice; it is a journey towards balance, self-awareness, and holistic well-being. Founded by Certified Yoga Teacher Vaishnavi Gujarathi, VAISHVA YOG was created with the vision of making authentic yoga accessible to people from all walks of life. Guided by the principle of "Breathe • Balance • Become" our approach combines traditional yogic wisdom with practical techniques that support modern lifestyles.

“Whether you are beginning your yoga journey or deepening an existing practice,
VAISHVA YOG offers a welcoming space for growth, transformation, and mindful living.”
